Polytechnic/Diploma

Diploma in Mechanical Engineering

Course Description: Mechanical engineering is considered as one of the most popular and reputed fields in polytechnic course after 10th. This polytechnic course is a field of engineering that applies the principle of physics for design, manufacturing analysis, and maintenance of mechanical systems. Some of the popular subjects covered in this field are Manufacturing Process, Strength of Materials, Theory of Machines, Machine Design, Product Design, Material Science, etc. With this course after 1Oth, you will also learn about software like Catia, Ansys, Solid works, AutoCAD, etc.

Career Prospects after 10th: Polytechnic Career Prospects after 10th: Students pursuing this course after 10th can work in both private as well as in public sector companies such as BHEL, Indian Railways, ONGC, NTPC, TATA, Jindal, etc. They can also work in various colleges as Professors. Job profile after this polytechnic course includes Mechanical Engineer, Professor, Assistant Professors, Manufacturing Manager, etc. Once you complete your diploma in this polytechnic course, you can also pursue B.E or B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering.

Diploma in Civil Engineering

Course Description: Civil Engineering is a field of engineering that deals with the construction, design, and maintenance of the natural and physical built environment such as roads, canals and bridges. Some of the popular subjects covered in this polytechnic course after 10th are Planning, Drafting, estimation, surveying, costing, Construction technology, etc. In this field, you will also learn software like STAAD, Revit, and AutoCAD. This polytechnic course after 10th grade is an excellent opportunity.

Career Prospects after 10th: Students who complete this polytechnic course after 10th can go into eal-estate companies, architects companies, airportS and universities, tunnel engineering and water resources. These job profiles after 10th include assistant manager, quality control engineer, assistant field inspector to name a few. Students can also pursue B.E or B.Tech in Civil Engineering.

Diploma in Computer Engineering

Course Description: Computer Engineering is a field of engineering that aims to provide basic knowledge of computer engineering, computing, mathematical techniques to the students. Some of the popular subjects covered in this polytechnic course are Applied Mathematics, Digital Electronics, OOPs, Mobile Computing, Software Testing and Network Security. This is a comprehensive polytechnic course after 10th that students can go for.

Career Prospects after 10th: With digitization taking all over the world, there are numerous job opportunities available in this polytechnic course after 10th. Completing courses after 10th opens job opportunities in both private as well as in public areas such as MNCs, Defence, Aerospace Sectors, Academic Institutes, Agricultural Sectors, Healthcare Companies and Manufacturing Companies. Some job profile after completing this polytechnic course includes Technical Writer, Web Designer, Programmer, and System Analyst.

Diploma in Electrical Engineering

Course Description: Electrical Engineering is a polytechnic courses field of engineering that deals with the study of electrical, electromagnetism, electronics, power, electronics, signal processing & telecommunications. Some of the popular subjects covered in this polytechnic course after 10th standard are Electric Machines, Embedded Systems, Elements of Electrical engineering, Electric Installation, Transmission & Distribution, Power Electronics, etc. This is a good polytechnic course after 10th that students can go for.

Career Prospects after 10th: The students have various job opportunities in both private as well as in public areas such as colleges and universities, power plants and Telecommunications. Some job profile for this polytechnic course includes Electrical Design Engineer, CAD Engineer, Technical Trainer, and Junior Engineer. You can also set up your own electrical equipment shop. After completing this course after 10th, you can also pursue B.E or B.Tech in Electrical Engineering. You can also setup your won electrical shop.

Diploma in Chemical Engineering

Course Description: Chemical Engineering is a field of engineering that deals with the study of bringing together Life Science and Physical Science in order to convert raw materials into newer products. Some of the popular subjects covered in this polytechnic field are Applied Chemistry, Heat Transfer, Mass Transfer, Environmental Engineering, Chemical Technology, Industrial Stoichiometry, Chemical& Safety Hazards, etc. This is an excellent polytechnic course after 10th.

Career Prospects after 10th: Polytechnic students can apply in the Environment Sector, Research Laboratories, Production, Petroleum Refineries, Academic Institutions and Ministry of Defence. You can also start a chemical laboratory in this course after 10th.

Diploma in Automobile Engineering

Course Description: Automobile Engineering is a field of engineering that includes elements of Mechanical Engineering, Electronic Engineering, Electrical engineering, Software Engineering, etc. In this polytechnic course, you can learn about the Automobile and its market analysis, customer relationships, sales and management. Some of the popular subjects covered in this polytechnic field are Applied Mathematics, Automobile Machine Shop, Automotive Engines, C Programming, Vehicle Maintenance, Thermal Engineering, and Strength of Materials.

Career Prospects after 10th: Polytechnic students can apply to TATA, Mahindra, Bajaj, Hero Moto Corp, Ford, etc. Job profile includes Automobile Designers, Automobile Technicians, Quality Engineer, Senior Production Engineer, Car Mechanics and Inspection Engineer, etc. After completing your diploma, you can also pursue B.E or B.Tech in Automobile Engineering. Students can also start their own automobile firm as a part of this polytechnic course after 10th.
