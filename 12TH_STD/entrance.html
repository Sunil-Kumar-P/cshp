Entrance Exams

1. JEE Main JEE Main stands for "Joint Entrance Examination Main ". This exam is an entrance exam for B.E./B.Tech and B.Arch/B.Planning. Paper 1 and paper 2 are two papers of JEE Main. The paper 1 is an entrance exam for B.E./B.Tech and paper 2 is an entrance exam for B.Arch/B.Planning. The JEE Main is organized by the National Testing Agency.

2. JEE Advanced JEE Advanced stands for "Joint Entrance Examination Advanced ". Students are eligible for JEE advanced after qualifying JEE Main exam. IIT is the conducting authority of the JEE Advanced. Paper 1 and paper 2 are the 2 papers in JEE Advanced. The paper is conducted in 2 shifts.

3. VITEEE Vellore Institute of Technology Engineering Entrance Examination is the full form of VITEEE. The exam is organized by the Vellore Institute of Technology, and this is an entrance exam for to take admission to VIT institute.
